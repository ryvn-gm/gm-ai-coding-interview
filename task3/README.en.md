# Task 3 — Verbal System Design

> 🌐 **Language:** English｜[繁體中文版](README.md)

This task is verbal — no coding required. Just a conversation.

## The task

We're integrating our smart ring SDK into a third-party health app. Their engineer said they'd like every detected rep pushed to their server in real time, via webhook. Please design how this integration would work.

## Format

- Verbal discussion, no laptop needed
- Feel free to sketch on paper or a whiteboard if it helps you think

## A few quick reminders

(See [welcome.en.md](../welcome.en.md) for the longer version.)

- **Think out loud.** This is a verbal task, so please share your reasoning, your questions, and your assumptions as you go. Sitting in silence for two or three minutes makes it really hard for us to follow you.
- **If something's unclear, make an assumption and build on it.** Try something like: *"I'll assume the third party really cares about not missing data more than sub-second latency — based on that, I'd…"* Then keep going. Please don't wait for us to clarify every detail before you start.
- **Don't know something? Just say so.** Something like *"I'm not super familiar with BLE — can I think out loud about it for a moment?"* is totally fine. We'd much rather hear that than watch you guess.

Good luck — and try to have a bit of fun with it!
